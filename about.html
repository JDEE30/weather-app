This simple web app was made for three purposes.


1. To allow it's users to quickly find current weather in their area.

2. To allow those users to also quickly find any clothes or items they may need for said weather.

3. Secure users easily using rss feeds to promote passive seo and quickly build an online audience.


What technologies are being used?


1. I decided to use the Darksky api to build this weather app due to it's ease of use and wide range of support.

2. I am using surfingWaves free feed tool for seo.
